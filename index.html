talyvn

Workforce management, redefined. The talyvn mark is built from three geometric primitives that interlock to form an abstract "t" letterform conveying precision, flow, and modern energy.

01

Logo Variants

The talyvn logomark ships in two treatments. Each is optimized for different contexts — use the right one to keep the brand sharp and consistent.

Flat

High Contrast & Digital-First

Bold saturated color blocks with clean hard edges. Maximum clarity at small sizes and on screen.

FaviconsApp IconsUI ElementsSocial Media
Glossy

Luminous Glass Treatment

Soft translucent surfaces with rim lighting and depth. Creates visual impact in hero areas and premium contexts.
